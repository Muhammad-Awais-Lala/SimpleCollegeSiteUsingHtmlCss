Introduction

Physical education at the college level is an academic discipline and practical training program that promotes physical fitness, wellness, and healthy lifestyle choices among students. It encompasses a wide range of activities, including individual and team sports, fitness training, and wellness education. College-level physical education aims to develop students' physical competencies, enhance their understanding of the benefits of regular physical activity, and foster a lifelong commitment to health and fitness. This comprehensive approach not only improves physical well-being but also supports mental health, teamwork, and discipline, contributing to the overall development of well-rounded individuals prepared for the demands of daily life and their future careers.
