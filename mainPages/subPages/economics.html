Welcome to Bs Economics

Introduction

The Bachelor of Science (BS) in Economics is an undergraduate degree that provides students with a solid foundation in economic theory, quantitative methods, and applied economics. This program is designed to equip students with the analytical skills and knowledge required to understand and analyze economic issues, make informed decisions, and solve real-world problems.
